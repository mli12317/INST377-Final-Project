Making Art More Accessible

Explore curated arts in an interactive format.

Project Overview

Problem Statement: Museums have thousands of artworks, but having digital access to these collections can feel overwhelming or difficult to navigate. Many people have issues accessing these art collections in an easily accessible and organized way. While the Art Institute of Chicago provides a public API, the raw data is not shown in a way that is easily navigable.

We aim to make museum data more usable and interactive for users and provide digital access to art collections.

Stakeholders include but are not limited to, art students, teachers, and the general public.
