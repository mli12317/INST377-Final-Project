Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How do I search for an artwork?

Use the search bar at the top of the homepage to enter keywords such as an artist's name or an artwork title.

How do I filter results?

Use the sidebar filters to narrow results by artist and classification.

What does the Classification Pie Chart mean?

The Classification Pie Chart indicates the total number of art classifications the museum holds.
